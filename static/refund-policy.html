💰 Refund Policy

Simple, fair, and transparent. No games.

✅ 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee — No questions asked.

1. Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request it within 14 days of your first payment
  • You haven't used more than 50% of your monthly AI resume scoring quota
  • You haven't referred more than 5 users (to prevent abuse)

2. How to Request a Refund

Email refunds@imusinfosystems.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Reason for refund (optional, but helps us improve)
  • Payment reference (from Razorpay receipt)

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

3. Prorated Refunds

For annual subscriptions cancelled after 14 days but within 30 days:

  • Refund = Annual fee minus 1 month at monthly rate
  • Example: ₹9,999 annual - ₹999 = ₹9,000 refunded

No refunds for cancellations after 30 days on annual plans. You retain access until the end of the billing period.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Partner/affiliate earnings (already paid out to partners)
  • Referral bonuses redeemed as credits
  • Custom enterprise contracts (handled separately)

5. Contact

Questions about refunds? Email us at refunds@imusinfosystems.com
