🚀 Getting Started

How do I create an account?▼
Click "Get Started Free" on the homepage. Enter your name, email, and phone. You'll get instant access to the Free plan. No credit card required.
What happens after I sign up?▼
Our AI onboarding wizard will guide you through: 1) Setting your job preferences, 2) Uploading your resume for scoring, 3) Configuring your notification preferences. The whole process takes under 3 minutes.
Can I use CareerOS without paying?▼
Yes! The Free plan includes resume scoring, 3 AI job matches per month, and access to our interview prep library. Upgrade to Pro or Elite anytime for unlimited features.

💳 Billing & Plans

How do I upgrade my plan?▼
Go to Settings → Subscription. Select your plan and complete payment via Razorpay. Your upgrade is instant — no waiting.
What's the refund policy?▼
We offer a 7-day full money-back guarantee. Not satisfied? Contact support within 7 days of your upgrade for a complete refund — no questions asked.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.

🤖 AI Features

How does the AI resume scoring work?▼
Upload your resume PDF or DOCX. Our AI analyzes it across 6 dimensions: ATS compatibility, keyword density, format, achievements, skills match, and clarity. You get a score out of 100 plus actionable suggestions.
How accurate is the job matching?▼
Our AI matches jobs based on your skills, experience, salary expectations, and preferences. The match score (shown as %) is based on semantic analysis of job descriptions vs. your profile. 85%+ is a strong match.
Is my data safe?▼
Absolutely. We use SSL encryption, store data in India (compliant with IT Act 2000), and never sell your data. Your resume is only used for scoring and job matching — never shared with third parties without consent.

⚙️ Account

How do I change my email?▼
Go to Settings → Profile. Click "Edit" next to your email. You'll receive a verification link at the new address. Click it to confirm the change.
How do I delete my account?▼
Go to Settings → scroll to Danger Zone → "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data. This action cannot be undone.
