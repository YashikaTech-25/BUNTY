🍪 Cookie Policy

How CareerOS uses cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, keeping you logged in, and understanding how you use our platform.

Types of Cookies We Use

CategoryPurposeDuration
EssentialAuthentication, security, session managementSession / 24h
PreferencesDark mode, language, notification settings1 year
AnalyticsPage views, feature usage, performance metrics2 years
MarketingReferral tracking, campaign attribution30 days

Third-Party Cookies

We use limited third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Razorpay — Payment processing and fraud prevention
  • SendGrid — Email delivery and tracking pixels

Managing Your Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Our Cookie Consent Banner — customize which categories you accept
  • Your Browser Settings — block or delete cookies at any time
  • Incognito/Private Mode — browse without persistent cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies will prevent login and core functionality.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive, you have the right to:

  • Know what cookies are being used
  • Give or withdraw consent for non-essential cookies
  • Request deletion of cookie data
