Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

1. Introduction

CareerOS ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Professional information (resume, skills, experience, salary expectations)
  • Job preferences and search history
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay)

2.2 Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Application tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered job matching and resume scoring
  • Send relevant job alerts and notifications
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Improve our AI algorithms and platform features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers in India, compliant with the Information Technology Act, 2000. We use SSL encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay) for transaction processing
  • Analytics providers (anonymized data only)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Export your data (available in Settings)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request account deletion

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for platform functionality and optional analytics cookies. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.
