🔒 Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, and password when you register.
  • Profile Information: Job preferences, skills, experience, location, and salary expectations.
  • Resume & Documents: Files you upload for AI scoring and job matching.
  • Usage Data: Jobs you view, applications you submit, and features you interact with.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Razorpay. We do not store card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized job recommendations and AI matching scores.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Send transactional emails (application updates, interview reminders).
  • Improve our AI models and platform features.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing.
  • SendGrid — for email delivery.
  • Analytics providers — anonymized usage data for platform improvement.
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission.
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt.
  • Admin tokens with role-based access control.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & IT Act)

  • Access: Download all your data from Data Export.
  • Correction: Update your profile anytime in Settings.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion — all data removed within 30 days.
  • Objection: Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable JSON/CSV formats.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for authentication, preferences, and analytics. You can manage cookie preferences via our Cookie Consent Banner. Essential cookies (login, security) cannot be disabled.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: All personal data removed within 30 days. Anonymized analytics may be retained.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years per tax regulations.

8. Children's Privacy

CareerOS is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email and posted on this page. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
