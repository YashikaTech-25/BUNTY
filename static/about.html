🚀 About CareerOS

Building the future of job searching — one AI match at a time.

Our Story

CareerOS was born in 2025 when our founders, fresh out of college, realized that job searching hadn't evolved in 20 years. Same job boards. Same resume black holes. Same anxiety.

We built CareerOS to change that. By combining AI resume scoring, intelligent job matching, and interview prep, we've helped 10,000+ professionals land their dream jobs — faster and with less stress.

We're not just another job board. We're your career co-pilot.
