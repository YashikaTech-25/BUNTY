🚀 Getting Started with CareerOS

Your first 48 hours with CareerOS. From signup to your first interview.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email or Google account. It takes 30 seconds. No credit card required for the free trial.

  • Choose a strong password (we'll check it for you)
  • Verify your email (check spam if you don't see it)
  • Complete your profile — the more detail, the better AI matching
→ Start Onboarding
2

Upload Your Resume

Drag and drop your resume (PDF or Word). Our AI will score it across 6 dimensions and tell you exactly what's working and what's not.

  • Score above 70? You're ready to apply.
  • Score below 70? Follow the AI suggestions to improve.
  • Re-upload after changes to see your score improve.
→ Upload Resume
💡 Pro Tip: Use our ATS-friendly templates from the Resources page if your score is low on "Format" or "ATS".
3

Set Your Job Preferences

Tell us what you're looking for so we can match you with the right opportunities.

  • Target roles (e.g., "Software Engineer", "Product Manager")
  • Preferred locations (remote, hybrid, on-site)
  • Expected salary range
  • Industry preferences
💡 Pro Tip: Be specific. "Software Engineer" gets 10x more matches than "Developer".
4

Get Matched & Apply

Our AI scans 1000+ job postings daily and matches you based on skills, experience, and preferences. Every match includes an AI match score.

  • 90%+ match? Apply immediately — high chance of success.
  • 70-89% match? Worth applying if the company interests you.
  • Below 70%? Use our resume optimizer to improve alignment.
→ Browse Jobs
5

Track Everything

Never lose track of an application again. Our Kanban tracker shows exactly where every application stands.

  • Move cards as you progress (Applied → Screening → Interview → Offer)
  • Add interview dates and prep notes
  • Set follow-up reminders
  • See your offer success rate
→ Open Tracker
6

Prep for Interviews

Use our Interview Prep tool to practice with AI-generated questions tailored to your target role.

  • 4 categories: Technical, Behavioral, Case Study, HR
  • STAR framework guidance for every answer
  • 2-minute timer to simulate real interview pressure
  • Toggle answers to self-check your responses
→ Start Practicing
💡 Pro Tip: Practice 5 questions daily. Consistency beats cramming.
